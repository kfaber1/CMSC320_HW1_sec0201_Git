Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging would preserve the contents of any branch that you want to merge to the one you work on. Rebasing will essentially rewrite your main branch with what the new commits of the merge branch. Rebasing creates a more linear version history while merging can create diamond shapes on your version history due to every branch being accounted for in a merge.
